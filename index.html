Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is PRIVADA?
PRIVADA is a social media platform designed to connect you with friends, family, and communities that share your interests. It allows you to share updates, photos, videos, and engage in discussions.
What makes PRIVADA different?
We focus on fostering authentic connections and providing a safer online environment. We prioritize user privacy and mental well-being above all else.
Is PRIVADA free to use?
Yes, PRIVADA is free to download and use. We may offer optional premium features in the future, but core functionality will always remain free.

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?
Download the app and follow the on-screen instructions to create your account.
How do I edit my profile?
Go to your profile page and tap "Edit Profile" to update your name, bio, profile picture, and cover photo.
How do I reset my password?
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen, answer security questions, and follow the reset instructions.

Using the App

How do I find friends?
Search for their name or username in the search bar, or connect through your contact list.
How do I post content?
Tap the "+" button on the main screen to create a new post with text, photos, videos, and other content.
How do I send private messages?
Go to someone's profile, tap "Invite", and once accepted, you can send messages through the messaging interface.

Privacy & Security

How is my data protected?
We take data security seriously. PRIVADA does not collect user data - only geolocation is collected. See our Privacy Policy for details.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Tap the "Report" button on any post or profile that violates our community guidelines.
How do I block someone?
Go to their profile and tap "Block" to prevent them from seeing your content and contacting you.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing
Try restarting the app or device. Ensure you have the latest version installed. Contact support if problems persist.
I'm not receiving notifications
Check notification settings in both the app and your device settings to ensure notifications are enabled for PRIVADA.
